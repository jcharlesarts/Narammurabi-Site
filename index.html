Hello there. Stay a while and listen!

Welcome to the workshop of Narammurabi, where ancient empires, trade routes, dynasties, and bad decisions become classroom games.

To some, I am known as King Narammurabi, but others call me Mister Pharaohmore Naramore.

The name is negotiable. The mission is not: build silly simulations, interactive readings, and quick study tools that make history a little less dull.

My students seem to approve. They replay the disasters, test their luck again, and keep asking for one more round.
